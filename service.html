Our Hospital Services

At our hospital, we are committed to providing comprehensive healthcare services to meet the
diverse needs of our community. Our dedicated team of professionals ensures top-quality care across a wide range of medical specialties.

Our Services

Service Description Available Locations
Emergency Care 24/7 urgent and emergency medical services with rapid response teams. Main Emergency Department
Cardiology Heart health assessments, diagnostics, and treatments including catheterizations. Cardiology Department, Second Floor
Radiology & Imaging Advanced imaging services such as MRI, CT scans, X-rays, and ultrasounds. Imaging Center, Ground Floor
Laboratory Services Comprehensive blood tests, pathology, microbiology, and diagnostics. Laboratory Department, First Floor
Pediatrics Child healthcare services, vaccinations, and pediatric consultations. Pediatric Wing, Third Floor
Orthopedics Bone, joint, and muscle care including surgeries and physiotherapy. Orthopedic Department, Third Floor
Oncology Cancer diagnosis, chemotherapy, and supportive care services. Oncology Center, Fourth Floor
Mental Health Psychiatric and psychological services including counseling and therapy. Mental Health Department, Fifth Floor
